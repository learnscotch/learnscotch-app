Other whisky traditions

🇮🇪 Irish whiskey

Triple-distilled for smoothness. No peat. Lighter and approachable. Key names: Jameson, Redbreast, Green Spot, Teeling.

🇺🇸 Bourbon

American whiskey, at least 51% corn, aged in new charred oak. Sweet, vanilla-forward. Key names: Buffalo Trace, Maker's Mark, Wild Turkey, Four Roses.
